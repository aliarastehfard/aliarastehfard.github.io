title: "Fingerprinting Attacks on Topics API"
date: 2025-12-04
---

Early thoughts on the fingerprinting risks that arise when user interests are reduced to a small,
coarse-grained set of topics. The attack surface expands when topics are combined with IP-based
signals and cross-site identifiers, letting adversaries recover more precise user profiles than
intended. A longer write-up with experiments is on the way.
